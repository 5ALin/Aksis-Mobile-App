**Aksis Mobile App:**

Aksis is a specialized mobile application designed to streamline project management and communications for field teams. By leveraging AI to triage emails and automate task creation, Aksis ensures you stay focused on what matters most.

Key Features
📬 Smart Daily Summaries & Task Management
Aksis automatically scans your inbox to summarize daily communications into actionable items.

Urgent Triage: Categorizes critical issues like emergency service dispatches or schedule impacts.

Action Needed: Highlights specific deadlines and coordination tasks.

Email Triaging: Displays how many emails were triaged, handled, or require your direct attention.

🤖 AI-Powered Chat & Automation
The integrated "Aksis Chat" serves as a project assistant that can draft and send professional communications.

Drafting & Sending: Request drafts for complex RFIs or project updates and send them directly from the chat interface.

Workflow Integration: Handles routine administrative responses so you can focus on field operations.

🛡️ Enterprise-Grade Security
Keep your project data safe with a dedicated security suite that monitors all incoming communications for threats.

Threat Detection: Identifies and blocks phishing, social engineering, and impersonation attempts.

Data Leak Prevention: Actively filters and redacts sensitive strategy leaks.

Security Logs: Provides a detailed threat log and statistics on scanned, blocked, and flagged items.

🏗️ Project & Team Tracking
Stay on top of your entire portfolio and field crew in real-time.

Jobs Dashboard: Monitor active jobs, financial status, and project health (On Track vs. At Risk).

Team Status: View live status updates for field team members, including their current site location and activity.
